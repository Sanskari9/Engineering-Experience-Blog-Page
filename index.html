All Learning Experience in Engineering

Choosing Engineering College isn't easy. All over the India there is thousand of Engineering college. Mostly Students dream is to get admission in IIT, NIT,etc. But My Dream was to get the admission in MITS Gwalior. And Luckly I got the college.

Hi, My name is Santosh Kushwah.Currently, I'm pursuing B.tech in Electrical Engineering from MITS Gwalior.And I am the First Engineer in my Family. Here I want to share all my learning throughout the degree.I share my experiences from 1st year to till now and all the thing that i was exploring in my past.

  1. First Year | The Journey begins

    The first year was the year where the engineering journey begins. The first year was all about induction program, new environment,first midsem fear,Endsem exam tension,meet new people and much more,etc.

    I was the first bencher in my first year.And I was also caring about my classes, assignment,exams, notes. And from the first year, I met my friend and these peoples are wonderful. I learned from the first year that Mostly students don't focus to exploring the things they was only touched with the academics I was one of them. I think Students should have take participate in the event that organised by the different clubs of the colleges.The clubs will helps to make connections , interactions with new peoples, And In the clubs you worked with the team where you learn team work. Although Engineering Labs also work same.

    And from the mid of the 2nd Sem, The Corona virus was come. And the lockdown was happend. And We got the General promotion by the Govt.

  2. Second Year | The Online Joy

    My Second Year was all about Online.The things was included like Whatsapp grp, online classes, online assignment, online attendance and online exams,etc.

    Second year was all about exploring the new things.Improve your skills and do experiments what you like to do, and much more. In my Second year i was doing mobile photography,sketching and learn new things. And I join Gym also inspired by the backbenchers where i learn descipline, pushing your potential every week and confidence,etc.

    I think In the Second Year, Students should explorer the new skills, learn and try to learning new skills where you find your potential and much more.

  3. Third Year | The Best Part of Engineering

    My Third Year was all about the Implemention what i learned till that time.In third year, you definitely find what you want to do in life and Focus on the one that thing you want to do,etc.

    And from the third year, I focusing on coding bcoz i want to go IT sector. And I took advice from my friend bcoz he already doing work in the IT sector, then I started learn java from youtube And learn WebD. And from feb 2022 My third year was started offline. We took classes and labs offline and online both.

    I think In the Third year, Students should focus on one thing that you want to do and ignore all the voice from the outside bcoz you know what you do in that field.

  4. Final Year | The Emotions

    The final year is all emotions where reality hits you and all the illusion of mind destroying one by one. The final year starts with the placement season.

    Everyone starts preparing the company and the first company was coming our campus is Accenture. My Experience of Accenture was not so good. I am going to the Interview but I am rejected. I learn from the accenture is you want to do more practice for the interview and exam. I didn't practice for the interview that why i rejected. And This was my First Interview.

    I think Final year give you brief about life where you fail and then you learn from that failure and Improve yourself , work on yourself,etc.

Engineering the best experience of my life. Engineering gives me Exposure, friends, open mindset, overall development of my thinking and much more. Engineering makes a person better human beings that makes some changes in the society by their inovation for the society.
